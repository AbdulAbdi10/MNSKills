About MNSkills

MNSkills was created to help adults build the confidence and skills they need to succeed in a technology-driven world. We believe learning should be practical, supportive, and connected to real opportunities.

Our programs are designed for working adults, parents, career changers, and community members who want to strengthen their digital, AI, and job-ready skills without feeling overwhelmed.

We partner with local schools, nonprofits, and community organizations to make high-quality training accessible across Minnesota.

What Makes MNSkills Different

  • Plain-language explanations—no jargon.
  • Step-by-step practice with real tools.
  • Small, supportive learning environments.
  • Curriculum tailored to adult learners.
